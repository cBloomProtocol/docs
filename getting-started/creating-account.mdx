# Creating Your Account

Getting started with Bloom Protocol is simple and takes just a few minutes. This guide will walk you through the entire process, from wallet connection to profile setup.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, you'll need:

### A Solana Wallet
Bloom Protocol supports multiple Solana wallets:
- **Phantom** (Recommended)
- **OKX Wallet** (Recommended)
- **Solflare**
- **Backpack**
- Other Solana-compatible wallets

<Info>
**New to Solana?** We recommend [Phantom Wallet](https://phantom.app) for its user-friendly interface and seamless integration with Bloom Protocol.
</Info>

### Some SOL for Gas Fees
You'll need a small amount of SOL (Solana's native token) for transaction fees:
- **Minimum recommended**: 0.1 SOL
- **For active users**: 0.5 SOL
- Transaction fees on Solana are typically less than $0.01

## Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

### Navigate to Bloom Protocol
1. Visit [bloomprotocol.ai](https://bloomprotocol.ai)
2. Click the **"Connect Wallet"** button in the top right corner

### Select Your Wallet
1. Choose your wallet from the list
2. Approve the connection request in your wallet
3. Sign the verification message to confirm ownership

<Warning>
**Security Tip**: Always verify you're on the official bloomprotocol.ai domain before connecting your wallet. Check for the secure lock icon in your browser.
</Warning>

## Troubleshooting

### Wallet Connection Issues

**Problem**: Wallet won't connect
- **Solution**: Refresh the page and try again
- Clear your browser cache
- Update your wallet extension
- Try a different browser

**Problem**: Transaction fails
- **Solution**: Ensure you have enough SOL for gas
- Check network congestion
- Try again in a few moments

### Profile Issues

**Problem**: Can't upload profile picture
- **Solution**: Ensure image is under 5MB
- Use JPG, PNG, or GIF format
- Check your internet connection

**Problem**: Username taken
- **Solution**: Try variations with numbers or underscores
- Use a unique combination
- Contact support if it's your brand name
