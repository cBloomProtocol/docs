## The Three Must-Haves

Every social mission submission requires three essential elements. Miss any one, and your submission won't be detected or approved.

### 1. Mission Account Mention

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="@bloom_mission" icon="at">

**What**: Include @bloom_mission in your post

**Why**: This is how our system discovers your submission when scanning X

**Format**: Type it exactly as `@bloom_mission` (lowercase, with underscore)

**Example Post**:

</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### 2. Project Team Mention

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Dev Team Account" icon="users">

**What**: Mention the project's X account specified in the mission details

**Why**: Connects your submission to the specific mission and notifies the project team

**Where to find it**: Look for "Dev Team" or "Project Account" in the mission details page

**Example from mission page**:
```
Mission by: Seedify (@SeedifyFund)
You must mention: @SeedifyFund
```

**Example Post**:
```

```

<Note>
Different missions have different project accounts. Always check the mission details for the correct account to mention.
</Note>

</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Content Quality Standards

### What Gets Approved

Your submission content is evaluated on authenticity and relevance:

| Quality Factor | What Reviewers Check | Example |
|----------------|---------------------|---------|
| **Originality** | Is this your own work? | Written in your voice, not copied |
| **Authenticity** | Did you actually do the task? | Specific details, screenshots, evidence |
| **Relevance** | Does it address the mission? | On-topic, follows requirements |
| **Effort** | Is it thoughtful? | More than generic praise or complaints |
| **Value** | Does it help the project? | Constructive feedback, real insights |

### The AI Content Rule

<Warning>
**Zero tolerance for AI-generated content**

Projects and reviewers can detect:
- ChatGPT/Claude writing patterns
- Generic AI responses
- Copy-pasted AI content
- Over-polished, unnatural language

**Result**: Instant rejection, possible account flagging

**Why**: Projects want genuine human feedback from real users who tested their product. AI content provides zero value.
</Warning>

## Submission Methods

### Automatic Detection (Recommended)

**How it works**:

<Steps>
  <Step title="You post on X">
    Include all three required elements (@bloom_mission, project team, your link)
  </Step>

  <Step title="System scans every 3 minutes">
    Automated service searches X for @bloom_mission mentions
  </Step>

  <Step title="Your post is discovered">
    System validates requirements and creates submission
  </Step>

  <Step title="Status updates automatically">
    Check mission page to see your submission appear
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Timeline**: Usually 3-10 minutes from posting

**Best for**: When you've followed all requirements exactly

**Verification**: Your post will appear in the mission's "Submissions" section

## Can I Submit Multiple Times?

### The Replacement Feature

**Yes! You can improve your submission before the deadline.**

This is one of the best features of social missions - you're not locked into your first attempt.

### How Replacement Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Post #1: First Attempt">
    You submit your initial post. System creates submission #1.
  </Step>

  <Step title="You Realize You Can Do Better">
    Maybe you found more insights, created better content, or got more thorough feedback.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Post #2: Improved Version">
    You post again with all required elements. System detects it.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Automatic Replacement">
    System sees it's the same user (UID), same Twitter account, different post.
    Submission #1 is replaced by submission #2.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Old Post Archived">
    Your first post is marked as "replaced by [new post]" in the system (audit trail).
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Replacement Rules

| Condition | Required? | Why |
|-----------|-----------|-----|
| Same UID | âœ“ Yes | Must be your profile link |
| Same Twitter account | âœ“ Yes | Can't switch accounts |
| Different post | âœ“ Yes | Must be a new post |
| Same mission | âœ“ Yes | Replaces within that mission only |
| Before deadline | âœ“ Yes | Can't replace after mission ends |

<Check>**You can**: Submit as many improved versions as you want before deadline</Check>
<Check>**You can**: Replace even after initial approval (if deadline hasn't passed)</Check>
<Check>**System automatically**: Keeps only your latest submission</Check>

<Warning>
**You cannot**:
- Submit from multiple Twitter accounts for the same mission
- Use different profile links (UIDs) for the same mission
- Replace after the mission deadline has passed
- Submit the same exact post URL multiple times
</Warning>

### Why This Matters

> **Quality is encouraged, not punished**
>
> The replacement feature means:
> - No penalty for experimenting
> - Iterate based on what you learn
> - Submit when you're proud of it, not when you're rushed
> - Your best work counts, not your first work

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Top Submission Failures

| Mistake | Why It Fails | How to Fix |
|---------|--------------|------------|
| **Protected Twitter account** | System can't read posts | Make account public |
| **Wrong mention format** | @bloom-mission vs @bloom_mission | Use underscore, not dash |
| **Missing project mention** | Can't match to mission | Check mission page for correct account |
| **No profile link** | Can't send payment | Include bloomprotocol.ai/your-uid |
| **AI-generated content** | Detected as inauthentic | Write yourself, show real usage |
| **Generic content** | No value to project | Be specific, show real testing |
| **Copy-pasted requirements** | Not original work | Create unique content |

### Red Flags Reviewers Look For

<Warning>
**Instant rejection signs**:
- Copy-pasted text from other submissions
- AI writing patterns (too polished, generic)
- No evidence of actual product usage
- Generic praise without specifics
- Spam-like content or bot behavior
- Violates mission-specific rules
</Warning>
