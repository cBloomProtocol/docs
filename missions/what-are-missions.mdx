## Mission Mechanics

### The One-Shot Rule

```
Mission Lifecycle:
Available ‚Üí In Progress ‚Üí Completed ‚Üí Claimed ‚Üí Historical
    ‚Üì            ‚Üì            ‚Üì           ‚Üì          ‚Üì
```

**No repeats. No resets. No second chances.** Each mission is a unique moment in time.

| Warning Sign | What It Means | Your Move |
