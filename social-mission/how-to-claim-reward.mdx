Social missions use X402 protocol for instant, direct payments. No claiming, no forms, no fees. Your public profile link becomes your payment address, and rewards flow automatically when approved.

## Reward Types

### Bloom Protocol Drops

<Accordion title="Water Drops (Platform Points)" icon="droplet">

**What they are**: Bloom Protocol's native reward system

**How you earn them**: Complete missions, contribute to platform, help projects

**How they're distributed**:
- Auto-credited to your Bloom Protocol account
- Appear in your dashboard balance instantly
- No wallet setup needed
- No blockchain transaction required

**What you can do with them**:
- Track your contribution history
- Build reputation on platform
- Unlock platform benefits
- Future utility (stay tuned)

**Icon**: Gradient water drop (teal to green)

</Accordion>

### Partner Token Rewards

<Accordion title="USDT, Project Tokens, etc." icon="coins">

**What they are**: Cryptocurrency tokens from mission sponsors

**Common types**:
- **USDT** (Tether stablecoin)
- **USDC** (USD Coin stablecoin)
- **Project tokens** (native tokens from the project)

**How they're distributed**:
- Sent directly to your wallet via X402
- Usually within minutes of approval
- Goes to the wallet linked to your X402 profile link
- Real cryptocurrency you fully own

**What you can do with them**:
- Trade on exchanges
- Hold as investment
- Use in DeFi protocols
- Transfer to other wallets
- Convert to fiat currency

**Requirements**:
- Must have X402 link set up and active
- Wallet must support the token's blockchain
- Profile link must be public and working

</Accordion>
